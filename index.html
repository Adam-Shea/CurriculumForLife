We're making content to teach young people essential skills and information!

Curriculum for life is a project created by the Thurrock Youth Cabinet after research showed that young people more than ever wanted to be taught skills that would prepare them for life. As a response, we created Curriculum for life [C4L], a program that enables young people to create content for other young people to learn from. Within this site, you'll be able to find lots of material that can be used to teach young people or for young people themselves to learn from.
